FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I own the photos that PixelShot generates? ▾
Yes, absolutely. All images generated through PixelShot are yours to use commercially. You can use them on your store, social media, ads — anywhere you need.
What if I'm not happy with the results? ▾
We offer a 100% money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with the quality, contact us within 7 days and we'll issue a full refund — no questions asked.
What kind of product photos should I upload? ▾
Upload 5–10 clear photos of your product from different angles. Any background works. Natural light or indoor light are both fine. Avoid heavy blur or extreme dark shots for best results.
What file formats are accepted? ▾
We accept JPG, PNG, WEBP, HEIC, TIFF, and GIF files. Maximum 20 MB per image. You can upload from your phone, computer, or directly from cloud storage.
How long does it take to receive my photos? ▾
Most orders are completed within 1–4 hours depending on your plan. Priority queue orders (Growth and Pro plans) are typically delivered within 1–2 hours. You'll receive an email notification when your photos are ready.
Is my data secure? What happens to my original photos? ▾
Your privacy is important to us. Original uploaded photos are automatically deleted from our servers within 24 hours of your order completion. We never use your images to train AI models or share them with third parties.
Is payment secure? ▾
Yes. All payments are processed through Stripe, a PCI Level 1 certified payment provider. We never store your card details. Your transaction is fully encrypted and secure.
How many of the generated photos will be usable? ▾
Typically 70–80% of generated photos are high quality and usable. With the Starter plan (40 images), you can expect 28–32 excellent photos. More styles means more variety and more usable options overall.
